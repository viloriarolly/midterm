ISPSC Delegates attend 6th Microbiome R&D, Business Collaboration Congress

Tokyo, Japan-–The delegations of the Ilocos Sur Polytechic State College headed by Dr. Gilbert R. Arce, SUC President III, attended the 6th Microbiome R&D and Business Collaboration Congress on April 26-27, 2023. This congress is also co-located with Asia 5th Probiotics & Prebiotics Congress, Asia 4th Skin Microbiome & Cosmeceuticals Congress, and joint probiotics symposium ’23. The said event is organized by Global Engage Ltd, and co-hosted with the Japanese Society of Probiotic Science (JSPS). Dr. Remy Sanidad (ISPSC’s Vice President for Public Information, Research, and Extension) and Ms. Charlene A. Ballesteros (faculty researcher) also attended the said congress. “The event will embrace fundamental and applied aspects scientifically and commercially including topic interests ranging from microbiome communities, how the microbiome affects health, disease, and the therapeutic industry, skin-based research, cosmetic advancements, probiotics, prebiotics postbiotics, brain health, academic-commercial partnerships, among others,” Yasuhiro Koga, President of Japanese Society for Probiotic Science, mentioned on his posted letter of invitation.#
